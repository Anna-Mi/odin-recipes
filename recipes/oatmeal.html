Oatmeal


Description

This creamy oatmeal is a warm, comforting start to your day. It's quick to make, filling, and easy to customize with your favorite toppings. Perfect for busy mornings or a cozy breakfast at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cup milk or water
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: honey, fruits, nuts, or cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Bring milk or water to a boil.
  2. Add oats and salt.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Remove from heat, cover, and let sit for 2 minutes.
  5. Top with honey, fruits, or nuts before serving.
