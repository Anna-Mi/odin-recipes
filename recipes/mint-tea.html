Mint Tea


Description

This refreshing mint tea is soothing, aromatic, and easy to make. It's perfect for relaxing after a meal or winding down in the evening. Enjoy it hot or chilled for a naturally calming drink.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 5-6 fresh mint leaves (or 1 tsp dried mint)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • 1 slice of lemon (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a kettle or pot.
  2. Place mint leaves in a cup or teapot.
  3. Pour hot water over the leaves and steep for 5 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea and add honey or lemon if desired.
  5. Serve hot or let cool and enjoy over ice.
