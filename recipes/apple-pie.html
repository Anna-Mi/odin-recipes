Apple Pie


Description

This classic apple pie is sweet, buttery, and filled with warm cinnamon-spiced apples. It’s the perfect dessert for family gatherings or cozy weekends. Serve it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for the ultimate treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pie crusts (store-bought or homemade)
  • 6 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix apples, sugar, cinnamon, flour, and lemon juice.
  3. Place one crust in a pie dish and fill with the apple mixture.
  4. Dot with butter, then cover with the second crust and seal the edges.
  5. Cut small slits in the top and bake for 40–45 minutes, until golden brown.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving.
